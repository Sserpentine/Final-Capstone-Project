Conserving Climate

What is Climate change?
Acording to NASA, climate change is a long-term change in the average weather patterns on Earth. This causes result in droughts, floods, cyclones and tornadoes. It is becoming warmer in the poles. This is causing the ice caps to melt and the sea levels to rise. Many islands are going underwater. This is also causing polar bears to lose their homes. In the areas where it is dry and hot there are forest and bush fires. This is causing many native animals like the koala bear go near extiction.
